About Radha Rani

Radha Rani, the embodiment of devotion and love, is revered as the eternal consort of Lord Krishna. Her life and stories teach us the essence of unconditional love and devotion.

Vrindavan - The Land of Krishna

Vrindavan, the sacred land of Krishna, is a place where the divine pastimes of Lord Krishna are said to have unfolded. It is the heart of Krishna devotion and an eternal abode of peace and love.

Barsana - Birthplace of Radha

Barsana, the birthplace of Radha Rani, is known for its spiritual significance, and the famous Lathmar Holi celebrations that symbolize Radha's playful love for Krishna.

Lord Krishna - The Divine Cowherd

Krishna, the supreme deity of love and compassion, is a symbol of divine playfulness, wisdom, and eternal devotion. His teachings in the Bhagavad Gita guide the hearts of millions.
